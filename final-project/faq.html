Frequently Asked Questions

What is Thakali Cuisine?

Thakali cuisine is a traditional style of Nepali food from the Mustang region. It is known for its balanced meals including rice, lentils, vegetables, pickles, and flavorful curries.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes! We offer a variety of vegetarian dishes including lentils, seasonal vegetables, pickles, and traditional items like dhido.

What are your most popular dishes?

Our Thakali set is the most popular, along with momo (dumplings), dhido, and traditional Nepali curries.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Yes, we offer takeout services. Please contact us directly for availability of delivery options.

Where are you located?

We are located in the local community area. Visit our Contact page for directions and more details.

About Us

SB Thakali is inspired by the traditional Thakali culture of Nepal, known for its balanced meals and rich flavors. Our goal is to bring authentic Nepali cuisine to our community while preserving its cultural roots.
