Welcome to SB Thakali Cuisine

Thakali food is one of Nepal’s best-known regional cuisines, admired for its balance, nourishment, and variety. Rooted in the traditions of the Thakali community of the Mustang region, it combines mountain ingredients, practical cooking methods, and deeply valued hospitality.

Experience the rich flavors of traditional Nepali cuisine at SB Thakali. Our restaurant is dedicated to serving authentic Thakali dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and time-honored cooking techniques.

From hearty rice and lentil sets to flavorful curries and handmade dumplings, every dish reflects the cultural heritage of Nepal. Whether you're new to Nepali food or a longtime fan, SB Thakali offers a warm and memorable dining experience.

Join us and explore a menu inspired by tradition, crafted with care, and served with genuine hospitality.
