History of Thakali Cuisine

Thakali cuisine comes from the Thakali community of the Mustang region in Nepal. Because the region lay along historic trade routes, the food culture developed with influences from local agriculture, mountain living, and cross-cultural exchange.

Traditional Origins

Early Thakali food traditions relied on locally available grains, lentils, yak or goat products, dried foods, and preserved ingredients suited to high-altitude life.

About Us

SB Thakali is inspired by the traditional Thakali culture of Nepal, known for its balanced meals and rich flavors. Our goal is to bring authentic Nepali cuisine to our community while preserving its cultural roots.
